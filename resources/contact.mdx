## Before Contacting Support

To help us resolve your issue quickly, please:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check the documentation">
    Many common questions are answered in our comprehensive guides:
    
    - [Frequently Asked Questions](/resources/faq)
    - [Troubleshooting Guide](/resources/troubleshooting)
    - [Feature Documentation](/features/dashboard)
    - [Extension Installation](/extension/installation)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Verify system status">
    Check if there's a known issue or ongoing maintenance:
    
    - Visit [status.applytrack.ai](https://status.applytrack.ai)
    - Check our Twitter [@applytrack](https://twitter.com/applytrack) for updates
    
    During maintenance windows, some features may be temporarily unavailable.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Gather relevant information">
    When contacting support, include:
    
    - **Account email**: The email associated with your ApplyTrack account
    - **Browser and version**: e.g., Chrome 120, Firefox 121
    - **Extension version**: Found in browser extension settings (e.g., v1.4.2)
    - **Operating system**: Windows 11, macOS Sonoma, etc.
    - **Steps to reproduce**: Detailed description of what you were doing when the issue occurred
    - **Screenshots or screen recordings**: Visual evidence of the problem
    - **Error messages**: Exact text of any error messages (if applicable)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Try basic troubleshooting">
    Quick fixes that resolve most issues:
    
    - Refresh the page (Cmd+R or Ctrl+R)
    - Clear browser cache and cookies
    - Sign out and sign back in
    - Update your browser to the latest version
    - Update the ApplyTrack extension to the latest version
    - Try accessing in an incognito/private window
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Support Request Template

When emailing support, use this template for faster resolution:

```text
Subject: [Brief description of issue]

Account Email: your-email@example.com
Plan: [Free / Premium Pro / Platinum Elite]

Issue Description:
[Detailed explanation of the problem]

Steps to Reproduce:
1. [First action taken]
2. [Second action taken]
3. [Result/error]

Expected Behavior:
[What you expected to happen]

Actual Behavior:
[What actually happened]

Environment:
- Browser: [e.g., Chrome 120.0.6099.109]
- Extension Version: [e.g., v1.4.2]
- Operating System: [e.g., macOS 14.2.1]

Screenshots/Attachments:
[Attach any relevant screenshots or files]

Additional Context:
[Any other relevant information]
```text
