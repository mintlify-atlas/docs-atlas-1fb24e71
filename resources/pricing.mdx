## Free Tier

**$0 per month**

The Free tier is perfect for casual job seekers or those just getting started with application tracking. It includes:

- **5 tracks per month** - Track up to 5 job applications monthly
- **Automated detection** - Our browser extension automatically captures application submissions on 200+ job boards
- **Standard dashboard** - Access your centralized career command center to view all tracked applications
- **No credit card required** - Start using ApplyTrack immediately without payment information

### Limitations

- AI Strategic Insights are not available on the Free tier
- Gmail/Outlook email synchronization is not included
- Limited to 5 application tracks per month

## Premium Pro

**$9 per month** (Recommended)

Our most popular tier for active job seekers. Premium Pro removes all tracking limits and adds powerful integrations:

### Core Features

- **Unlimited tracking** - Track as many job applications as you need with no monthly limits
- **Deep Gmail/Outlook Sync** - Secure OAuth integration tracks every email thread and automatically updates application states
- **AI Strategic Insights** - Weekly executive summaries analyzing your response rates with actionable suggestions to increase conversion
- **Prioritized support** - Get faster responses from our support team
- **Advanced Analytics** - In-depth performance metrics and trends for your job search

### Why Premium Pro?

This tier is recommended for job seekers actively applying to multiple positions. The unlimited tracking and email synchronization ensure you never miss an update, while AI insights help you optimize your application strategy based on real data.

## Platinum Elite

### Additional Features

### Who Should Choose Platinum Elite?

This tier is ideal for:

- Candidates preparing for high-stakes interviews

## Getting Started

### Sign Up

All users start with the Free tier:

1. Visit the [quickstart guide](/quickstart)
2. Create your account with email or use GitHub/Google OAuth
3. Install the browser extension
4. Start tracking applications immediately

**No credit card required for the Free tier.**

### Upgrading

Upgrade to Premium Pro or Platinum Elite at any time from your account dashboard:

1. Navigate to Settings > Billing
2. Select your desired tier
3. Enter payment information
4. Your upgrade takes effect immediately

## Billing

- All paid plans are billed monthly
- Cancel anytime from your account settings
- Downgrade to Free tier without losing your data
- Pro-rated refunds available within 14 days

## Frequently Asked Questions

  <Accordion title="What happens if I exceed 5 tracks on the Free tier?">
