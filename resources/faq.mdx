## Getting Started

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What is ApplyTrack and how does it work?">
    ApplyTrack is an intelligent job application tracking platform that automatically captures and manages your career pipeline. When you apply to jobs on supported platforms, our browser extension silently detects the submission and logs it to your dashboard—no manual data entry required.

    The system then syncs with your email inbox (Gmail or Outlook) to track recruiter responses, interview invitations, and offer letters, automatically updating your application statuses in real-time.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Which job boards and platforms are supported?">
    ApplyTrack supports automatic detection on 200+ job boards including:

    - **Major platforms**: LinkedIn, Indeed, Glassdoor, ZipRecruiter
    - **Tech-focused**: AngelList, Wellfound, Hired, Triplebyte
    - **Company career pages**: Most ATS systems like Greenhouse, Lever, Workday, and Taleo
    - **Startup boards**: Y Combinator jobs, RemoteOK, We Work Remotely

    Our content script is continuously updated to support new platforms as they emerge.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do I need to install anything to use ApplyTrack?">
    Yes, you'll need to install the ApplyTrack browser extension to enable automatic application detection. The extension is available for:

    - **Chrome**: Chrome Web Store
    - **Firefox**: Firefox Add-ons
    - **Edge**: Microsoft Edge Add-ons

    After installing the extension, sign in with your ApplyTrack account credentials. The extension runs in the background and only activates when you're on a supported job board.

    [Install the extension →](/extension/installation)
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I use ApplyTrack without the browser extension?">
    The browser extension is required for automatic application detection. However, you can manually add applications directly through the dashboard if you prefer not to use the extension, or if you're applying through platforms that aren't yet supported.

    Manual entry allows you to track applications from email submissions, networking referrals, or any custom workflow.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Extension & Auto-Tracking

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How does the extension detect job applications?">
    The ApplyTrack extension uses a high-speed content script that monitors DOM events on supported job boards. When you click "Submit" or "Apply," the extension captures:

    - Company name and job title
    - Application date and time
    - Job description and requirements  
    - Application URL and platform metadata
    - Location and salary information (if available)

    All data is encrypted and sent to your private dashboard within seconds.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Does the extension slow down my browser?">
    No. The extension is optimized for minimal resource usage and only activates on job board domains. It uses lazy-loading and efficient event listeners to ensure your browsing experience remains fast and responsive.

    The extension typically uses less than 5MB of memory and has no impact on page load times.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Will the extension work on all websites?">
    The extension only activates on supported job boards and application platforms to respect your privacy and minimize resource usage. It will not track or monitor activity on other websites.

    You can view the full list of supported platforms in your dashboard settings or contact our team to request support for a specific platform.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I temporarily disable the extension?">
    Yes, you can disable the extension at any time through your browser's extension settings. When disabled, ApplyTrack will not capture new applications automatically, but your existing data and dashboard access remain unchanged.

    You can also configure the extension to only track applications on specific domains through the extension settings panel.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Email Sync & Integration

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How does email sync work?">
    ApplyTrack uses secure OAuth authentication to connect to your Gmail or Outlook account. We never store your email password—authentication is handled directly by Google or Microsoft.

    Once connected, ApplyTrack monitors email threads related to your job applications and automatically updates statuses when it detects:

    - **Recruiter responses** → Status changes to "In Review"
    - **Interview invitations** → Status changes to "Interviewing"  
    - **Rejection emails** → Status changes to "Not Selected"
    - **Offer letters** → Status changes to "Offer Extended"

    [Learn more about email sync →](/features/email-sync)
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What email providers are supported?">
    Currently, ApplyTrack supports:

    - **Gmail** (including Google Workspace accounts)
    - **Outlook** (including Microsoft 365 accounts)

    Support for additional providers like ProtonMail and custom IMAP servers is planned for future releases.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is my email data secure?">
    Absolutely. ApplyTrack takes email security seriously:

    - **OAuth authentication**: We never store your email password
    - **Encrypted storage**: All email metadata is encrypted at rest using AES-256
    - **Minimal access**: We only read job-related threads, not your entire inbox
    - **Local processing**: Email content is analyzed using locally-hosted models, not sent to third parties
    - **GDPR compliant**: Full data deletion available on request

    [Read our privacy policy →](https://applytrack.ai/privacy)
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I disconnect my email at any time?">
    Yes, you can revoke ApplyTrack's access to your email at any time through your dashboard settings. Disconnecting will:

    - Stop all future email monitoring
    - Revoke OAuth tokens immediately
    - Preserve your existing application data (unless you choose to delete it)

    You can reconnect your email later without losing any historical data.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Data & Privacy

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How is my application data stored?">
    All application data is stored in secure, encrypted databases hosted on enterprise-grade infrastructure:

    - **Encryption at rest**: AES-256 encryption for all stored data
    - **Encryption in transit**: TLS 1.3 for all API communications
    - **Isolated storage**: Your data is logically separated from other users
    - **Regular backups**: Automated daily backups with 30-day retention
    - **SOC 2 compliant**: Annual third-party security audits
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I export my application data?">
    Yes, you can export all your data at any time in multiple formats:

    - **CSV**: Import into Excel or Google Sheets
    - **JSON**: Machine-readable format for custom integrations
    - **PDF**: Formatted report with all application details

    Navigate to **Settings → Data Export** to generate and download your export file.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I delete my account and data?">
    To permanently delete your account:

    1. Go to **Settings → Account**
    2. Scroll to **Danger Zone**
    3. Click **Delete Account**
    4. Confirm deletion (this action is irreversible)

    Upon deletion:
    - All application data is permanently removed within 48 hours
    - Email sync connections are revoked immediately
    - Browser extension access is disabled
    - All subscriptions are canceled with no refunds

    We recommend exporting your data before deletion.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do you sell or share my data with third parties?">
    **No, never.** ApplyTrack does not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes. Your application data, email content, and career information remain strictly confidential.

    We only share data when:
    - Required by law (e.g., valid subpoena)
    - Necessary for service operations (e.g., hosting providers with strict NDAs)
    - You explicitly authorize sharing (e.g., exporting data)

    See our [Privacy Policy](https://applytrack.ai/privacy) for complete details.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Dashboard & Features

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What metrics does the dashboard track?">
    The ApplyTrack dashboard displays:

    - **Total Applications**: Count with month-over-month growth percentage
    - **Interviews**: Number of interviews scheduled and completed
    - **Offer Rate**: Percentage of applications resulting in offers
    - **Active Pipeline**: Applications currently in discussion
    - **Response Rate**: Percentage of applications receiving recruiter responses
    - **Time-to-Response**: Average time from application to first recruiter contact

    Premium users also see AI-powered insights comparing your metrics to industry benchmarks.

    [Explore the dashboard →](/features/dashboard)
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I manually edit application details?">
    Yes, you have full control over all application data. You can:

    - Edit company name, job title, location, salary
    - Update application status manually
    - Add custom notes and tags
    - Upload related documents (resume, cover letter)
    - Set interview dates and reminders
    - Archive or delete applications

    Click any application card to open the detail view and make changes.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do AI insights work?">
    ApplyTrack's AI engine (available on Premium and Platinum plans) analyzes your application data to provide:

    - **Response rate analysis**: Identify which job types, companies, or platforms yield the best response rates
    - **Timing optimization**: Suggest optimal days and times to submit applications
    - **Profile recommendations**: Detect patterns in successful vs. unsuccessful applications
    - **Opportunity alerts**: Notify you of new job postings matching your profile
    - **Strategic guidance**: Weekly summaries with actionable next steps

    All AI processing uses locally-hosted models to protect your privacy.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I access ApplyTrack on mobile?">
    Yes, the ApplyTrack dashboard is fully responsive and works on mobile browsers. Simply visit your dashboard URL from any device and log in.

    We're currently developing native iOS and Android apps with offline support and push notifications. Join the waitlist in your dashboard settings to be notified when they launch.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
