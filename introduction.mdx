<div className="mt-16 mb-16 max-w-5xl mx-auto px-6">
  <h2 className="text-2xl sm:text-3xl font-bold dark:text-white text-gray-900 mb-4">Quick Start</h2>
  <p className="text-base dark:text-gray-400 text-gray-600 mb-8">Get up and running with ApplyTrack in minutes</p>
  
  <Steps>
    <Step title="Create your account">
      Sign up at [applytrack.ai](https://github.com/philtechphilips/applytrack) and verify your email. Choose a plan that fits your job search needs—our Free tier includes 5 tracked applications per month.
      
      <Note>
        All plans include automatic application detection and the standard dashboard. Premium features like AI insights and unlimited tracking are available on paid plans.
      </Note>
    </Step>
    
    <Step title="Install the browser extension">
      Download the ApplyTrack extension for Chrome, Firefox, or Edge from your browser's extension store. Once installed, sign in with your account credentials.
      
      The extension runs silently in the background and automatically detects when you submit job applications on supported platforms like LinkedIn, Indeed, Glassdoor, and 200+ other job boards.
    </Step>
    
    <Step title="Apply to your first job">
      Visit any supported job board and apply to a position as you normally would. When you click "Submit" or "Apply," ApplyTrack automatically captures:
      
      - Company name and job title
      - Application date and time
      - Job description and requirements
      - Application URL and metadata
      
      <Accordion title="Example: Captured Application Data">
        ```json
        {
          "company": "Tesla",
          "role": "Senior Software Engineer",
          "status": "Applied",
          "appliedAt": "2026-03-04T10:30:00Z",
          "platform": "LinkedIn",
          "location": "Palo Alto, CA",
          "salary": "$140k - $180k",
          "url": "https://linkedin.com/jobs/..."
        }
        ```
      </Accordion>
    </Step>
    
    <Step title="Connect your email (optional)">
      Link your Gmail or Outlook account to automatically track recruiter responses, interview invitations, and offer letters. ApplyTrack uses secure OAuth authentication and never stores your email password.
      
      Once connected, the system monitors threads related to your applications and updates statuses in real-time—from "Applied" to "Interviewing" to "Offer Extended."
      
      [Learn more about email sync →](/integrations/gmail)
    </Step>
    
    <Step title="Monitor your dashboard">
      View your complete career pipeline at a glance. The dashboard displays:
      
      - **Total applications** with month-over-month growth
      - **Interview pipeline** with upcoming rounds
      - **Offer rate** compared to industry benchmarks
      - **Response rate** and time-to-reply metrics
      - **AI-powered insights** on optimization opportunities
      
      [Explore the dashboard →](/dashboard/overview)
    </Step>
  </Steps>
</div>
