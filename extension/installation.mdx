The ApplyTrack browser extension is your high-speed bridge between job boards and your career dashboard. Once installed, it automatically captures every application you submit—no manual data entry required.

## System Requirements

Before installing, ensure you meet these requirements:

- Chrome 90+, Firefox 88+, or Edge 90+
- Active ApplyTrack account (free or premium)
- Internet connection for real-time sync

<Note>
The extension works seamlessly across 200+ job boards including LinkedIn, Indeed, Glassdoor, and more. See the [Supported Platforms](/extension/supported-platforms) page for the complete list.
</Note>

## Installation Steps

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Chrome">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Visit Chrome Web Store">
        Navigate to the ApplyTrack extension page in the Chrome Web Store (search for "ApplyTrack").
      </Step>
      
      <Step title="Add to Chrome">
        Click the **Add to Chrome** button in the top right corner.
        
        A popup will appear asking for permissions. The extension requires:
        - Access to job board websites to detect applications
        - Storage permissions to sync with your dashboard
        - Network access to communicate with ApplyTrack servers
      </Step>
      
      <Step title="Confirm Installation">
        Click **Add Extension** to confirm the installation.
        
        The ApplyTrack icon will appear in your browser toolbar. You may need to click the puzzle piece icon to pin it for easy access.
      </Step>
      
      <Step title="Sign In">
        Click the ApplyTrack extension icon and sign in with your account credentials.
        
        The extension will sync with your dashboard and activate automatic tracking.
      </Step>
    </Steps>

## Verifying Installation

After signing in, you should see:

- A green checkmark on the extension icon indicating active status
- A welcome notification confirming connection to your dashboard
- Your current tracking plan (Free, Premium, or Platinum)

<Tip>
Pin the extension to your toolbar for quick access to tracking status and recent applications.
</Tip>

## Extension Permissions Explained

The ApplyTrack extension requests specific permissions to function:

| Permission | Purpose |
|------------|----------|
| **Active Tab** | Detects when you're on a job board and monitors application submissions |
| **Storage** | Caches application data locally before syncing to prevent data loss |
| **Web Request** | Identifies successful application submissions by monitoring network activity |
| **Host Permissions** | Accesses 200+ job board domains to inject the content script |

<Warning>
ApplyTrack uses high-grade encryption for all data transmission. Your application data is strictly yours and never shared with third parties.
</Warning>

## Troubleshooting

### Extension Icon Not Appearing

1. Check that the installation completed successfully in your browser's extensions page
2. Look for the puzzle piece icon (Chrome/Edge) or add-ons icon (Firefox) in your toolbar
3. Pin the ApplyTrack extension from the extensions menu
4. Restart your browser if the icon still doesn't appear

### Cannot Sign In

1. Verify you have an active ApplyTrack account at [applytrack.ai/login](https://applytrack.ai/login)
2. Check your internet connection
3. Clear browser cache and cookies, then try again
4. Disable other extensions temporarily to check for conflicts
5. Try signing in through the main dashboard first, then refresh the extension

### Extension Shows Offline Status

1. Check your internet connection
2. Verify the ApplyTrack service status at [status.applytrack.ai](https://status.applytrack.ai)
3. Sign out and sign back in to refresh the connection
4. Check if your browser is blocking third-party connections
5. Temporarily disable VPN or proxy settings that might interfere

### Not Detecting Applications

1. Ensure you're on a [supported platform](/extension/supported-platforms)
2. Verify the extension icon shows green (active) status
3. Check that you've completed the entire application submission (clicked final "Submit" button)
4. Some job boards may require additional time to process—check your dashboard in 1-2 minutes
5. Review the [Usage Guide](/extension/usage) for platform-specific instructions

### High Memory Usage

1. The extension uses minimal resources, but multiple open job board tabs can increase memory usage
2. Close unused job board tabs
3. Clear cached data from the extension settings
4. Restart your browser

### Extension Conflicts

1. Disable other job-related extensions temporarily
2. Check for ad blockers or privacy extensions that might interfere with application detection
3. Whitelist ApplyTrack domains in your security extensions

<Note>
Still having issues? Contact support at [support@applytrack.ai](mailto:support@applytrack.ai) or visit our [troubleshooting guide](/resources/troubleshooting).
</Note>

## Updating the Extension

The ApplyTrack extension updates automatically:

- Chrome and Edge check for updates every few hours
- Firefox updates extensions daily
- Manual update: Visit your browser's extensions page and click "Update" if available

<Tip>
We recommend keeping the extension up-to-date to ensure compatibility with the latest job board changes and security improvements.
</Tip>

## Uninstalling

To remove the extension:

1. Navigate to your browser's extensions page
2. Find ApplyTrack in the list
3. Click **Remove** or **Uninstall**
4. Confirm removal

<Warning>
Uninstalling the extension will stop automatic tracking, but your existing application data in the dashboard remains intact. You can reinstall at any time to resume tracking.
</Warning>
