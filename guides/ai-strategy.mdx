## Overview

ApplyTrack's AI engine analyzes your application patterns, market trends, and company behaviors to provide strategic insights that help you land more interviews and offers. Available on Premium and Platinum plans, our AI features transform your job search from reactive to proactive.

## AI Opportunity Engine

The AI Opportunity Engine monitors job postings in real-time and alerts you to high-priority opportunities that match your profile.

### How It Works

The engine analyzes:
- Your tech stack and skill set from previous applications
- Companies you've shown interest in
- Application timing windows and competition levels
- Job posting freshness and urgency signals

### Real-Time Opportunity Alerts

When the AI detects a strategic match, you'll see alerts in your dashboard:

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/ai-opportunity.png" alt="AI Opportunity Alert" />
</Frame>

**Example Alert:**
> "OpenAI just posted 3 new roles that perfectly match your tech stack. Submit before the application cap to be in the first 10% of candidates."

The system highlights:
- **Perfect matches** based on your skills and experience
- **Timing advantages** like application caps or early posting windows
- **Action items** with one-click deployment to start tracking

### Taking Action

When you see an opportunity alert:

1. Click **Deploy Now** to open the job posting
2. The browser extension will automatically track your application
3. AI monitoring begins for recruiter responses and next steps

## Maximizing AI Features

### Best Practices

**Keep Data Fresh:**
