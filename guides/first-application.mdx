# Track Your First Application

Get started with ApplyTrack by tracking your first job application. This guide walks you through the entire process from installation to viewing your application in the dashboard.

## Prerequisites

Before you begin, ensure you have:
- An active ApplyTrack account
- Chrome, Firefox, or Edge browser
- Access to any of the 200+ supported job boards (LinkedIn, Indeed, Glassdoor, etc.)

## Installation

<Steps>
  <Step title="Install the Browser Extension">
    Navigate to your browser's extension store and search for "ApplyTrack" or visit our website to download the extension directly.
    
    The extension will appear in your browser toolbar with the ApplyTrack icon.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Connect Your Account">
    Click the ApplyTrack extension icon and sign in with your credentials. The extension will automatically connect to your dashboard.
    
    <Tip>
      Pin the extension to your toolbar for easy access. You'll see a small badge notification whenever an application is successfully captured.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Apply to Your First Job">
    Navigate to any supported job board and complete a job application as you normally would:
    
    1. Fill out the application form
    2. Upload your resume
    3. Click the "Submit" or "Apply" button
    
    The moment you submit, ApplyTrack's high-speed content script automatically detects and captures:
    - Company name
    - Job title/role
    - Application date and time
    - Job board source
    - Application URL
    
    <Note>
      No manual data entry required! The instant capture technology works in the background, so you can focus on crafting great applications.
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="View Your Application in the Dashboard">
    Open your ApplyTrack dashboard at `app.applytrack.ai/dashboard` to see your first tracked application.
    
    Your application will appear in the "Recent Activity" section with:
    - Company logo initial (e.g., "A" for Anthropic)
    - Role title
    - Status badge showing "Applied"
    - Timestamp (e.g., "2h ago")
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Understanding Application Status Progression

Your applications automatically progress through these statuses:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Applied" icon="briefcase">
    Initial status when you submit an application. This appears immediately after the extension captures your submission.
    
    **What to expect:** Your application enters the company's ATS (Applicant Tracking System). Response time varies by company.
    
    **Dashboard display:** Grey badge with "Applied" label
  </Step>

  <Step title="Interviewing" icon="clock">
    Status updates automatically when email sync detects interview scheduling communications.
    
    **What to expect:** You'll receive interview invitations via email. ApplyTrack monitors these threads and updates the status.
    
    **Dashboard display:** Blue badge with "Interviewing" label
    
    **Example:** Anthropic - AI Researcher shows this status in the dashboard after interview scheduling
  </Step>

  <Step title="Technical Round" icon="code">
    Specific interview stage indicating technical assessment phase.
    
    **What to expect:** Coding challenges, system design interviews, or technical presentations.
    
    **Dashboard display:** Amber badge with "Technical Round" label
    
    **Example:** Tesla - Senior Developer shows this status for technical assessments
  </Step>

  <Step title="Offer Extended" icon="sparkles">
    Final successful status when the company extends a job offer.
    
    **What to expect:** Formal offer letter, compensation details, and benefits information.
    
    **Dashboard display:** Emerald green badge with "Offer Extended" label
    
    **Example:** Scale AI - Product Designer displays this status after offer acceptance
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Viewing Your Application Journey

Once your first application is tracked, explore these dashboard sections:

### Total Applied Stats

- **Current count:** See your total applications (e.g., 142 tracked)
- **Growth metric:** "+12% month over month" shows your application velocity
- **Icon:** Briefcase icon in blue

<Tip>
  Maintain consistent application volume. The platform benchmarks show successful job seekers apply to 10-15 positions per week.
</Tip>

### Active Pipeline

- **Current count:** Applications in active discussion (e.g., 18 active)
- **Meaning:** Applications past initial screening, requiring follow-up
- **Icon:** Lightning bolt (Zap) icon in indigo

### Recent Activity Timeline

Your dashboard displays the most recent applications with:
- Company name and role
- Current status
- Time elapsed since submission
- Quick access to application details

## Common Questions

### What if the extension doesn't detect my application?

The extension automatically detects submissions on 200+ job boards. If an application isn't captured:
1. Ensure the extension is enabled and you're logged in
2. Check that you're on a supported job board
3. Manually add the application using the "New Track" button in Quick Commands

### Can I edit application details?

Yes! Click any application in your Recent Activity list to edit:
- Company name
- Role title
- Status
- Application notes
- Custom tags

### How many applications can I track?

- **Free tier:** 5 tracks per month
- **Premium Pro:** Unlimited tracking
- **Platinum Elite:** Unlimited + advanced features
