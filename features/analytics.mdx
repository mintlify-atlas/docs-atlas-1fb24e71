## Export & Reporting

Generate reports for personal tracking or career coaching:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="CSV Export">
    
    Export all applications with fields:
    - Company name
    - Role title
    - Application date
    - Current status
    - Last update timestamp
    - Response time (if applicable)
    - Salary range
    - Notes
    
    Perfect for importing into spreadsheets or sharing with career advisors.
    
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="PDF Reports">
    
    Generate formatted reports:
    
    - **Weekly summaries**: Application activity and insights
    - **Monthly performance**: Trends, graphs, and benchmark comparisons
    - **Custom date ranges**: Analyze specific time periods
    - **Company-specific**: All applications to a single employer
    
    Includes charts, graphs, and AI commentary on your performance.
    
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Share Links">
    
    Create shareable dashboard links (Platinum only):
    
    - **Read-only access**: Share your application portfolio with career coaches
    - **Time-limited**: Links expire after 7 days
    - **Privacy controls**: Hide sensitive data like salary expectations
    
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
