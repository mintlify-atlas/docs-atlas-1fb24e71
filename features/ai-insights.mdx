## How AI Insights Work

<Steps>
  <Step title="Data Collection">
    
    The AI analyzes multiple data sources:
    
    - **Your applications**: Companies, roles, tech stacks, success rates
    - **Email history**: Response times, interview conversion patterns
    - **Resume/profile**: Skills, experience level, education
    - **Market data**: Job posting velocity, hiring trends, salary benchmarks
    - **Company signals**: Funding rounds, growth indicators, hiring urgency
    
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pattern Recognition">
    
    Machine learning models identify:
    
    - Which companies have high response rates for your profile
    - Optimal application timing (day of week, time of day)
    - Tech stack matches that lead to interviews
    - Application volume thresholds ("first 10% of candidates")
    - Follow-up timing that maximizes response rates
    
  </Step>
  <Step title="Real-Time Recommendations">
    
    Insights appear in your dashboard as actionable cards:

## Privacy & AI Models

<Info>
  ApplyTrack uses **locally-hosted AI models** for text processing. Your data is never sent to third-party AI APIs like OpenAI or Anthropic.
</Info>

### Our AI Stack

- **On-device models**: Run on your device for sensitive data (resume analysis)
- **Private cloud**: Hosted on our secure infrastructure for compute-intensive tasks
- **No training**: Your data is never used to train AI models
- **Explainable AI**: All recommendations include reasoning and data sources

### Data Usage

The AI analyzes:
- Application history (companies, roles, outcomes)
- Email content (recruiter messages only)
- Resume/profile text
- Public job market data

It **does not** access:
- Emails unrelated to job applications
- Personal files or documents
- Browsing history outside job boards
- Social media posts

## Disabling AI Features

You can disable AI insights while keeping other Premium features:

1. Go to Settings → Privacy
2. Toggle **"AI Insights"** off
3. Your data will no longer be analyzed, but tracking and email sync continue

<Warning>
  Disabling AI will remove opportunity recommendations, weekly summaries, and interview prep features.
</Warning>
