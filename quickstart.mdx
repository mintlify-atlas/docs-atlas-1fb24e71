Welcome to ApplyTrack, your intelligent career tracking engine. This guide will walk you through creating your account and tracking your first job application.

<Note>
ApplyTrack automatically captures job applications from 200+ job boards and syncs with your email—no manual data entry required after initial setup.
</Note>

## Create Your Account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to the signup page">
    Visit the ApplyTrack signup page or click **Maximize My Search** from the homepage.

    You'll see the signup form with the heading "Initialize your career engine" and the tagline about joining 10,000+ high-performance candidates.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Complete the signup form">
    Fill in the following fields:

    - **First Name**: Your first name (e.g., "John")
    - **Last Name**: Your last name (e.g., "Doe")
    - **Work Email**: Your professional email address (e.g., "john@example.com")
    - **Choose Password**: A secure password (minimum 8 characters)

    <Tip>
    Look for the green checkmarks confirming:
    - No credit card required for free tier
    - Instant browser extension access
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Sign up with email or OAuth">
    Choose one of the following options:

    **Option 1: Email Signup**
    
    Click the blue **Start Free Deployment** button to create your account with email.

    **Option 2: Quick OAuth**
    
    Use the social login buttons below the divider that says "Or expedite with":
    - **Github**: Connect with your GitHub account
    - **Google**: Connect with your Google account

    <Note>
    By signing up, you agree to ApplyTrack's Terms of Protocol and Data Privacy.
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Access your dashboard">
    After successful signup, you'll be redirected to your **Dashboard Overview**.

    Your dashboard displays:
    - Welcome message: "Welcome back, [Your Name]. You have 3 interviews coming up this week."
    - Four stat cards showing:
      - **Total Applied**: Track all applications
      - **Interviews**: Monitor scheduled rounds
      - **Offer Rate**: Compare against industry average (1.8%)
      - **Active Pipeline**: Applications currently in discussion
    - Recent Activity feed
    - Upcoming Rounds sidebar
    - Quick Command action buttons
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Track Your First Application

ApplyTrack offers multiple ways to track job applications:

### Method 1: Automatic Browser Extension Capture

<Steps>
  <Step title="Install the browser extension">
    After signup, you have instant access to the ApplyTrack browser extension.

    The extension's content script automatically detects successful applications on 200+ job boards including:
    - LinkedIn
    - Indeed
    - AngelList
    - Greenhouse-powered career pages
    - Lever-powered career pages
    - And many more
  </Step>

  <Step title="Apply to a job on any supported platform">
    Navigate to your preferred job board and submit an application as you normally would.

    When you click "Submit Application" or equivalent, ApplyTrack's content script detects the submission.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Automatic capture confirmation">
    The extension automatically captures:
    - Company name
    - Role/position title
    - Application date and time
    - Job posting URL
    - Initial status: "Applied"

    <Note>
    No manual entry required. The application appears in your dashboard within seconds.
    </Note>
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Method 2: Manual Quick Add

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click the 'New Track' button">
    From your dashboard, locate the **Quick Command** section in the right sidebar.

    Click the **New Track** button (blue icon with briefcase).
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter application details">
    Fill in the manual entry form:

    - **Company Name**: e.g., "Anthropic"
    - **Role Title**: e.g., "AI Researcher"
    - **Application Date**: When you applied
    - **Job URL**: Link to the posting (optional)
    - **Status**: Select from dropdown (Applied, Interviewing, Offer, etc.)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save and track">
    Click **Deploy Track** to add the application to your pipeline.

    The application will appear in your Recent Activity feed and Applications list.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Method 3: Email Synchronization

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect your email">
    Click the **Sync Mail** button in the Quick Command section.

    ApplyTrack supports secure OAuth integration with:
    - Gmail
    - Outlook
  </Step>

  <Step title="Grant OAuth permissions">
    Authorize ApplyTrack to access your email threads.

    <Warning>
    ApplyTrack uses high-grade encryption and locally-hosted models for text processing. Your email data is never stored on external servers.
    </Warning>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Automatic email tracking">
    Once connected, ApplyTrack automatically:
    - Detects application confirmation emails
    - Tracks interview scheduling threads
    - Updates application status based on recruiter responses
    - Syncs offer letters and rejection notices

    The header badge will show: "Syncing 24 Active threads"
  </Step>
</Steps>

## View Your Applications

After tracking your first application:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check Recent Activity">
    Your dashboard's **Recent Activity** section displays your most recent applications.

    Each application card shows:
    - Company initial icon (e.g., "A" for Anthropic)
    - Company name and role title
    - Time since application (e.g., "2h ago")
    - Status badge (color-coded):
      - **Applied**: Gray
      - **Interviewing**: Blue
      - **Technical Round**: Amber
      - **Offer Extended**: Emerald
    - Quick actions menu (three dots)

    Example entry:
    ```
    [A] Anthropic
        AI Researcher
        2h ago | [INTERVIEWING] | •••
    ```
  </Step>

  <Step title="Navigate to full Applications list">
    Click **Applications** in the left sidebar to see all tracked applications.

    The Applications view includes:
    - Header showing "All (142)" and "Active (12)" with pulsing blue indicator
    - Search and Filter controls
    - Complete list of applications organized by date

    <Tip>
    Use the search bar at the top to filter by company name, role, or status.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Update application status">
    Click on any application card to:
    - View full details
    - Update status manually
    - Add notes and interview dates
    - Track follow-up actions

    Status options include:
    - Applied
    - Screening
    - Interviewing
    - Technical Round
    - Culture Fit
    - Offer Extended
    - Accepted
    - Rejected
  </Step>
</Steps>
